Film Capacitor

Film capacitors are principally used in power supplies for telecom equipment, automotive, medical and smart home appliances. They are also well suited for many general-purpose and industrial applications in electronic equipment. Film capacitors can be used for a number of different uses and applications, including:

  • UPS System
  • Solar Inverter
  • Automotive
  • Energy Meter
